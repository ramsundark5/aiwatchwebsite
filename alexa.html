Add Notify Me Alexa Skill

1. Enable the Notify Me skill from https://www.amazon.com/Thomptronics-Notify-Me/dp/B07BB2FYFS.
2. Next, just say, "Alexa, open Notify Me" and the skill will introduce itself and send your access code via email.
3. The email will come from from notifyme@notifymyecho.com via amazonses.com. Remember: launching the skill ("Alexa, open Notify Me" or "Alexa, launch the Notify Me skill") is what triggers the email with the access code.

Integrate aiwatch and Alexa

1. Open aiwatch mobile app.
2. Tap the Settings icon at the bottom of the screen.
3. Under Connect Alexa setting, enter the access token from Notify Me
4. Tap the toggle button next to Connect Alexa.
5. Congrats, aiwatch can now notify your preferred alerts directly to your Alexa device.
