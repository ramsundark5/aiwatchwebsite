Amazon Fire devices support

Amazon fire devices can run aiwatch and provides great value for the money. Though aiwatch can be installed in Amazon devices, to get all the features, you will need Google Play store to be installed.
Lifewire has a detailed article on how to install Google Play on Kindle Fire. Check it here https://www.lifewire.com/install-google-play-kindle-fire-4570988.
Once Google play is installed, download aiwatch from Google play https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.aiwatch
